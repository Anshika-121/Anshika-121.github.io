My graduation engineering project transcended the classroom — resulting in a published research paper on an innovative biomedical patch that monitors vital signs, aids prosthetic sensation, and detects jaundice non-invasively.

eSKIN: Adaptive Technology for Diverse Applications — A Multi-Functional Biomedical Flexible Patch for Vital Sign Monitoring, Prosthetic Sensation & Jaundice Detection
Anshika Tiwari et al. · UCER, Prayagraj · Peer-Reviewed Journal · 2025
This paper presents the design, fabrication, and clinical validation of a flexible biomedical patch integrating photoplethysmography (PPG), thermal sensing, and optical bilirubin detection on a single PCB substrate — enabling real-time, non-invasive health monitoring across diverse applications.
